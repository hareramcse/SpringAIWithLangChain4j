IT Help Desk Policies — Random Technologies

Password Reset Policy
- Users can reset passwords via the self-service portal at https://password.randomtechnologies.com.
- If the self-service portal is unavailable, contact the help desk with your employee ID and registered email.
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character.
- Passwords expire every 90 days. Users receive reminders 14 days before expiration.
- After 5 failed login attempts, the account is locked for 30 minutes.
- For accounts locked longer than 30 minutes, contact IT help desk for manual unlock.

Equipment Request Policy
- New equipment requests must be submitted through the IT portal at https://it.randomtechnologies.com/request.
- Standard equipment (keyboard, mouse, headset) is shipped within 3 business days.
- Laptops and monitors require manager approval and are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Equipment older than 4 years is eligible for refresh. Submit a refresh request through the portal.
- Damaged equipment must be reported within 48 hours with photos of the damage.
- All company equipment must be returned upon termination or role change.

Software Installation Policy
- Pre-approved software can be installed from the company Software Center.
- Non-standard software requires approval from IT Security and the user's manager.
- Submit software requests at https://it.randomtechnologies.com/software with business justification.
- Approved software requests are processed within 2 business days.
- Installing unauthorized software on company devices is a policy violation.

Remote Work IT Support
- Remote workers must use the company VPN for all work-related activities.
- VPN client download: https://vpn.randomtechnologies.com/download.
- Home internet minimum requirement: 25 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload.
- For home office equipment issues, create a help desk ticket with priority based on impact.
- If unable to work due to IT issues, notify your manager and escalate the ticket to URGENT.

Ticket SLA (Service Level Agreement)
- URGENT: Initial response within 30 minutes, resolution within 4 hours.
- HIGH: Initial response within 1 hour, resolution within 8 hours.
- MEDIUM: Initial response within 4 hours, resolution within 24 hours.
- LOW: Initial response within 8 hours, resolution within 48 hours.
- SLA timers run during business hours only (Mon-Fri, 8 AM - 6 PM EST).
- If an SLA is about to be breached, the ticket is automatically escalated.

Account Access and Permissions
- New employee accounts are provisioned within 24 hours of start date.
- Access to shared drives, applications, and tools is based on role and department.
- Additional access requires approval from the resource owner and IT Security.
- Terminated employee accounts are disabled within 2 hours of HR notification.
- Contractor accounts expire automatically after the contract end date.
